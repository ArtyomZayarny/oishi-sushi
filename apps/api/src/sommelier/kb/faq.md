# Frequently asked questions

Honest answers to common questions. Where something is not on our menu, the
correct answer is to say so plainly and point the guest back to the menu — never
to invent a dish, a price, a discount, or a service we do not offer.

## Ordering & cart

**Can I add a recommended dish to my cart?** Yes — every dish the sommelier
suggests can be added to your cart in one click from its card.

**Can the sommelier order or pay for me?** No. The sommelier recommends dishes
and adds them to your cart; you review the cart and check out yourself.

## What we serve

**What kind of food is this?** We are a sushi shop. Everything we serve is on the
menu — nigiri, maki rolls, sashimi, a donburi rice bowl, a chef’s omakase course,
and a set for two.

**Do you have a tasting menu?** The Chef’s Omakase is a chef-selected course of
twelve pieces; the Couple’s Set is a larger shared selection for two.

**Do you have vegetarian or vegan options / ramen / tempura / pizza / dessert?**
If an item is not shown on the menu, we do not currently offer it, and the
sommelier will say so rather than guess. The honest response to "do you have X?"
when X is not on the menu is: we don’t have that — here is what is on the menu
instead. The sommelier should never name a dish that is not on the menu.

## Freshness & sourcing

**How fresh is the fish?** Our fish is sourced for same-day quality. The Chef’s
Omakase is chosen by the chef each morning and delivered cold-chain; the Otoro
Selection is bluefin belly aged five days for flavor; the Sashimi Moriawase is
cut from the morning’s best fish. Specific sourcing beyond what a dish’s
description states is best confirmed with the shop directly rather than assumed.

## What’s new

**What’s new / what just got added?** The sommelier knows which dishes are the
newest additions to the menu and can recommend them when you ask "what’s new?".
It determines this from the live menu, not from this document, so its answer
always reflects the current menu.

## Deals & discounts

**Do you have any deals, discounts, coupons, or special offers?** We do not have
any offers or promotions to share through the sommelier. There is no discount
code, loyalty deal, or promotional bundle for it to offer. The honest answer to
any "any deals?" / "discount code?" / "what’s on special?" question is that there
are none available here, with a pointer to browse the full menu. The sommelier
must never invent an offer, a coupon code, or a price reduction.
