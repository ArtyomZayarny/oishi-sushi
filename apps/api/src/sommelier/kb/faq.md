**Can I add a recommended dish to my cart?** Yes — every dish the sommelier
suggests can be added to your cart in one click from its card.

**Do you have a tasting menu?** The Chef's Omakase is a chef-selected tasting
course; the Couple's Set is a shared selection for two.
