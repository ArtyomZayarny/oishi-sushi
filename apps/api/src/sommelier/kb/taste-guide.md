Fattier cuts of tuna eat rich, buttery, and melt-in-the-mouth; leaner cuts taste
cleaner and more savoury. On this menu, the Otoro Selection leans to the rich,
fatty end, while the Sashimi Moriawase offers a range of clean, delicate slices.
Roe dishes such as Ikura Don bring a briny, popping texture rather than a fatty
one.
